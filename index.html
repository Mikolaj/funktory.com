Funktory.com is the software engineering company founded by Mikolaj Konarski,
headquartered in Warsaw, Poland and distinguished by cooperation with Well-Typed,
the Haskell infrastructure industry leader.

Funktory.com is currently in the stealth startup mode, but during breaks in plotting world domination,
we do consulting, indie game development and experiments with the feedback loop
between procedural content generation (including content-driven AI) and player input
(data-mined play-style, level ratings, in-game created content).
Our current experimental platform is the free software game Allure of the Stars.

We are available for contract and consulting work concerning any problems
caused by or solvable with software. We strongly prefer Haskell
for the programming part of the work, if there is any. The more difficult and mission-critical
the problem, the higher the chance we are your best bet, up to, but not including,
impossible assignments, which we strive to diagnose and report early.
