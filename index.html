Functory is a start-up company in stealth mode founded by Mikolaj Konarski.

During breaks in plotting world domination, we do indie game development
and experiment with the feedback loop between procedural content generation
(including content-driven AI) and player input (data-mined play-style, level ratings,
in-game created content).

We are available for contract and consulting work concerning any problems
that are caused by or can be solved with software. We strongly prefer Haskell and OCaml
for the programming part of the work, if there is any. The more difficult
and mission-critical the problem, the higher the chance we are your best bet up to,
but not including, impossible assignments, which we try to reject early.
